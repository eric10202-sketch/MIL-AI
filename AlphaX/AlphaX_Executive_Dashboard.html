Project Overview

Project AlphaX is a full IT carve-out programme separating the Bosch Battery Division into a fully independent, standalone NewCo legal entity. Bosch acts as the Sponsor Contractor (Seller), currently owning and operating all Battery IT. The permanent strategic buyer is in sourcing; in the interim, a dedicated NewCo legal entity is being established to receive the carved-out Battery business.

Unlike integration-model carve-outs, AlphaX has no Merger Zone concept. All Battery IT — including ERP (SAP), WAN, Active Directory, M365, ~200 applications, Engineering IT, Production OT systems, and HR IT — must be cleanly separated from the Bosch corporate environment and stood up exclusively in NewCo's own IT landscape by 1 October 2027 (Day 1). Bosch operates a Transitional Service Agreement (TSA) as the sole bridge for any Battery systems not yet separated at Day 1.

The programme spans 19 months (June 2026 – December 2027) across ~35 Battery sites and ~3,000 users in EMEA, AP, and AM regions. KPMG leads methodology and PMO across 5 quality-gated phases.

Key Risk Indicators — Pre-Project Assessment
▲ HIGH RISK — NewCo Legal Entity (TBC)
Permanent buyer not confirmed. NewCo legal entity must be established for interim operation. All architecture decisions must remain revertible. ERP and IAM systems must not be buyer-specific until sourcing is resolved.
P:4 · I:4 · Rating: 16
▲ HIGH RISK — WAN Lead Time (4–6 Months)
Network orders for 35 Battery sites must be placed no later than QG2 (Nov 2026) to avoid Day 1 go-live delay. Dual-vendor pre-qualification must begin in Phase 1. Critical path dependency for all site cutovers.
P:3 · I:5 · Rating: 15
▲ HIGH RISK — SAP ERP Strategy Unresolved
Greenfield vs shell copy decision has significant cost and timeline implications. Shell copy aligns with FRAME benchmark (9–12 months SIT cycle). Decision must be made at QG1 to keep Phase 3 on schedule.
P:3 · I:4 · Rating: 12
◆ MEDIUM — IT Security / IAM · No CISO Appointed
No interim NewCo CISO confirmed. ISO 27001 baseline pre-assessment blocked. IAM platform selection not started. Bosch CISO policies apply during TSA — security architecture must be compliant from Day 1.
P:3 · I:4 · Rating: 12
◆ MEDIUM — 19-Month Compressed Timeline
Stand Alone model with full stack build in 19 months is aggressive. Requires parallel workstream execution from kick-off with no sequential gating. Resource conflicts must be tracked from Phase 1.

Country-Specific Complexity Hotspots

🇩🇪 Germany (HQ)

Battery HQ and primary data centre. Co-location hub decision impacts all EMEA sites. Works council involvement required for IT changes affecting employees. GDPR data residency obligations.

🇨🇳 China (AP)

Local FTS / customs systems require local hosting. Great Firewall constraints impact cloud architecture. China PIPL data localisation rules apply. Local IT partner engagement required early in Phase 2.

🇮🇳 India (AP)

RBIN (Restricted Bosch Internal Network) policy must be formally waived for NewCo connectivity. India-specific network architecture review required. RBIN exception process must start Phase 1.

🇲🇽 Mexico (AM)

Legal entity registration delays are common. Tax authority lead times (SAT) add 6–12 weeks. ERP localisation for Mexican tax compliance (CFDI) must be scoped by QG2.
