Stand Alone Model — Key Differences

vs Integration model (Trinity benchmark)
⚠ No Merger Zone
Battery IT goes directly to NewCo environment. No hybrid interim state. All systems must land in NewCo on Day 1 or be under explicit TSA.
⚠ NewCo Builds from Zero
NewCo has no existing IT. All platforms — WAN, AD, M365, ERP, IAM, ITSM — must be procured and built within 19 months.
⚠ Buyer TBC
Permanent buyer not confirmed. All NewCo decisions must be revertible. Architecture must not be buyer-specific until sourcing completes.
✓ Compressed Timeline
19 months vs 42 for Trinity. Enabled by smaller scope (35 sites, ~200 apps) and a focused Battery-only landscape.
