PROJECT OVERVIEW

Eagle represents a stand-alone carve-out of the Seller's Security Camera Business, establishing an independent IT operating environment for 300 users across 3 manufacturing sites in Singapore. The carve-out enables strategic independence for the Buyer to modernize application infrastructure (SAP MM migration, legacy WMS consolidation) and pursue cloud-native architecture post-implementation. TSA-supported hypercare extends 90 days post-Go-Live (Nov 8, 2026 - Feb 6, 2027).
